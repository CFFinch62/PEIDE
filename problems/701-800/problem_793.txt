Problem 793: Median of Products

Let $S_i$ be an integer sequence produced with the following pseudo-random number generator: $S_0 = 290797$ $S_{i+1} = S_i ^2 \bmod 50515093$ Let $M(n)$ be the median of the pairwise products $ S_i S_j $ for $0 \le i \lt j \lt n$. You are given $M(3) = 3878983057768$ and $M(103) = 492700616748525$. Find $M(1\,000\,003)$.
