Problem 530: GCD of Divisors

Every divisor $d$ of a number $n$ has a complementary divisor $n/d$. Let $f(n)$ be the sum of the greatest common divisor of $d$ and $n/d$ over all positive divisors $d$ of $n$, that is $f(n)=\displaystyle\sum_{d\mid n}\gcd(d,\frac n d)$. Let $F$ be the summatory function of $f$, that is $F(k)=\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^k f(n)$. You are given that $F(10)=32$ and $F(1000)=12776$. Find $F(10^{15})$.
