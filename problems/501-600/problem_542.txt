Problem 542: Geometric Progression with Maximum Sum

Let $S(k)$ be the sum of three or more distinct positive integers having the following properties: No value exceeds $k$. The values form a geometric progression. The sum is maximal. $S(4) = 4 + 2 + 1 = 7$ $S(10) = 9 + 6 + 4 = 19$ $S(12) = 12 + 6 + 3 = 21$ $S(1000) = 1000 + 900 + 810 + 729 = 3439$ Let $T(n) = \sum_{k=4}^n (-1)^k S(k)$. $T(1000) = 2268$ Find $T(10^{17})$.
